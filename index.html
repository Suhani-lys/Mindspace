What MindSpace Offers

Anonymous Sharing

Post your thoughts, feelings, and experiences freely. No name, no profile picture, no judgment.

AI Emotion Analysis

Our NLP model reads the emotional context of your posts and provides personalized, supportive responses.

Mood Tracker

Log your daily emotional state and visualize patterns over time with a clean, private dashboard.

Community Support

Connect with others who understand. Upvote helpful posts, offer support, and feel less alone.

End-to-End Privacy

Zero PII stored. Your account is pseudonymous by design. We never sell or share your data.

Private Journal

Write entries only you can see. A quiet space to process your thoughts without external noise.
